Side by Side

Screws vs. Nails

Both have a place in your toolbox. Here's when to reach for each.

🔩 Screws

  • Strong holding power — resists pull-out force
  • Fully removable without damaging material
  • Best for precision and structural connections
  • Ideal for decks, cabinets, and furniture
  • Multiple head types for every application

🔨 Nails

  • Faster installation — nail guns speed things up
  • Better for framing and shear strength
  • Lower cost per fastener
  • Ideal for rough framing and sheathing
  • Flexibility resists splitting in green lumber
